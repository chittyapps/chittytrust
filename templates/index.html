Trust Dimension Analysis

Understanding the 6 Dimensions

Each dimension captures a crucial aspect of trustworthiness

Source (Who)

Identity verification, credentials, and professional certifications. Who are you really?

Temporal (When)

Time-based analysis, consistency, and behavioral patterns over time.

Channel (How)

Communication and transaction channels used. Blockchain beats anonymous.

Outcome (Results)

Track record of positive/negative outcomes. Results speak louder than promises.

Network (Connections)

Quality of network connections and endorsements. You are your network.

Justice (Impact)

Alignment with justice principles and positive societal impact. The ultimate measure.
