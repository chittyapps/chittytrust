How Trust Works

Understanding the 6D Trust Scoring System

What is Trust?

Trust in ChittyOS is measured across 6 key dimensions that evaluate different aspects of your digital identity and behavior. Each dimension contributes to your overall trust score, which ranges from 0-100 and determines your ChittyID trust level.

Trust Levels

  • L4 Institutional: 90-100 points
  • L3 Professional: 75-89 points
  • L2 Enhanced: 50-74 points
  • L1 Basic: 25-49 points
  • L0 Anonymous: 0-24 points

Output Scores

  • People Score: Social trust rating
  • Legal Score: Compliance & justice alignment
  • State Score: Institutional trust
  • ChittyScore: Overall composite score

The 6 Dimensions of Trust

1. Source Trust (15% weight)

WHO you are - Your identity verification and credentials

What builds it:

  • • Email verification (+30 points)
  • • Government ID verification (+20 points)
  • • Professional certifications (+5 each)
  • • Multiple credentials (+10 per credential)
  • • Biometric verification (+15 points)

Why it matters:

Proves you are who you claim to be. Higher verification levels unlock more features and build confidence with other users.

2. Temporal Trust (10% weight)

WHEN you act - Time-based consistency and longevity

What builds it:

  • • Account age (older = better)
  • • Regular activity patterns
  • • Consistent behavior over time
  • • Recent engagement
  • • Long-term positive history

Why it matters:

Shows stability and reliability. New accounts or inconsistent activity patterns may indicate higher risk.

3. Channel Trust (15% weight)

HOW you interact - Communication and transaction channels

Channel trust scores:

  • • Verified API: 95 points
  • • Blockchain: 90 points
  • • Bank transfer: 85 points
  • • Credit card: 80 points
  • • OAuth: 75 points
  • • Email: 60 points
  • • SMS: 55 points
  • • Social media: 40 points

Why it matters:

More secure channels indicate higher trust. Using multiple verified channels shows authenticity and reduces fraud risk.

4. Outcome Trust (20% weight)

RESULTS you deliver - Track record of positive outcomes

What builds it:

  • • Successful transactions
  • • Completed projects
  • • Positive feedback from others
  • • Dispute resolution success
  • • Meeting commitments

Why it matters:

Your actual performance matters most. Consistently positive outcomes build strong trust, while negative outcomes lower your score.

5. Network Trust (15% weight)

WHO trusts you - Your connections and endorsements

What builds it:

  • • High-trust connections
  • • Mutual verifications
  • • Professional endorsements
  • • Community participation
  • • Referrals from trusted users

Why it matters:

Social proof through trusted connections. Quality matters more than quantity - one L4 connection is worth more than many L1 connections.

6. Justice Trust (25% weight)

HOW FAIRLY you act - Alignment with justice and fairness

What builds it:

  • • Fair dispute resolutions
  • • Transparent communication
  • • Ethical behavior
  • • Following community guidelines
  • • Helping others resolve issues

Why it matters:

The highest-weighted dimension. Justice and fairness are core to trust. This dimension ensures the system rewards ethical behavior above all else.

How Your Score is Calculated

Composite Score Formula

ChittyScore =
  Source × 15% +
  Temporal × 10% +
  Channel × 15% +
  Outcome × 20% +
  Network × 15% +
  Justice × 25%

Output Scores

  • People Score: Network (40%) + Outcome (30%) + Temporal (30%)
  • Legal Score: Justice (50%) + Source (30%) + Channel (20%)
  • State Score: Source (40%) + Justice (40%) + Channel (20%)
  • ChittyScore: Weighted average of all 6 dimensions

Security & Privacy

What We Track

  • • Verification completion events
  • • Transaction outcomes (not details)
  • • Communication channel types
  • • Network connection quality
  • • Dispute resolution results

What We Don't Track

  • • Personal conversation content
  • • Financial transaction amounts
  • • Private documents content
  • • Location or device data
  • • Browsing behavior

Your trust score is calculated using privacy-preserving methods. We focus on verifiable outcomes and behaviors, not private data.
